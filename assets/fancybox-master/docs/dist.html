ABRIDGED PRESCRIBING INFORMATION:
SINGULAIR® Chewable Montelukast Tablets BP 4 mg, Chewable Montelukast Tablets BP 5 mg and Montelukast Sodium Tablets IP 10 mg. INDICATIONS: 1) Prophylaxis and chronic treatment of asthma in adults and pediatric patients of 12 months of age and older 2) Relief of symptoms of allergic rhinitis (seasonal allergic rhinitis in adults and pediatric patients 2 years of age and older and perennial allergic rhinitis in adults and pediatric patients 6 months of age and older).DOSE, METHOD OF ADMINISTRATION AND USAGE: Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet should be taken once daily. For asthma, the dose should be taken in the evening. For allergic rhinitis, the time of administration may be individualized to suit patient needs. Patients with both asthma and allergic rhinitis should take only one tablet daily in the evening. Dosage in Adults 15 Years of Age and Older with Asthma and/or Allergic Rhinitis: One 10-mg tablet daily. Dosage in Pediatric Patients 6 to 14 Years of Age with Asthma and/or Seasonal Allergic Rhinitis: One 5-mg chewable tablet daily. Dosage in Pediatric Patients 2 to 5 Years of Age with Asthma and/or Allergic Rhinitis: One 4-mg chewable tablet daily or one packet of 4 mg oral granules daily. Dosage in Pediatric Patients 12 months to 2 Years of Age with Asthma or 6 months to 2 years of age with perennial Allergic Rhinitis.: one packet of 4 mg oral granules daily. General Recommendations: Patients should be advised to continue taking Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet while their asthma is controlled as well as during periods of worsening asthma, No dosage adjustment is necessary for pediatric patients, for the elderly, for patients with renal insufficiency, or mild-to-moderate hepatic impairment, or for patients of either gender. USE IN SPECIAL POPULATION: In Pregnancy: Pregnancy Category B Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet has not been studied in pregnant women. Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet should be used during pregnancy only if clearly needed, Nursing Mothers: It is not known if Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet is excreted in human milk, Because many drugs are excreted in human milk, caution should be exercised when Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet is given to a nursing mother. Pediatric Use: Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet has been studied in pediatric patients 6 months to 14 years of age. Safety and effectiveness in pediatric patients younger than 6 months of age have not been studied. Studies have shown that Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet does not affect the growth rate of pediatric patients. Use in the Elderly: In clinical studies, there were no age-related differences in the efficacy or safety profiles of Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet. CONTRAINDICATIONS: Hypersensitivity to any component of this product. WARNING AND PRECAUTION: Oral Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet should not be used to treat acute asthma attacks as the efficacy has not been established. Patients should be advised to have appropriate rescue medication available. While the dose of concomitant inhaled corticosteroid may be reduced gradually under medical supervision, Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet should not be abruptly substituted for inhaled or oral corticosteroids. The reduction in systemic corticosteroid dose in patients receiving anti-asthma agents including leukotriene receptor antagonists has been followed in rare cases by the occurrence of one or more of the following: eosinoptlilia, vasculitic rash, worsening pulmonary symptoms, cardiac complications, and/or neuropathy sometimes diagnosed as Churg-Strauss syndrome, a systemic eosinophilic vasculitis. Although a causal relationship with leukotriene receptor antagonism has not been established, caution and appropriate clinical monitoring are recommended when systemic corticosteroid reduction is considered in patients receiving Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet. UNDESIRABLE SIDE-EFFECTS: Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet has been generally well tolerated. Side effects, which usually were mild, generally did not require discontinuation of therapy. The overall incidence of side effects reported with Montelukast Sodium tablets 4 mg, 5 mg chewable tablets and 10 mg film coated tablet was comparable to placebo. Adults 15 Years of Age and Older with Asthma: Only in ≥1% of patients, were abdominal pain and headache. Pediatric Patients 6 to 14 Years of Age with Asthma: Only in > 1% of patients, was headache. Pediatric Patients 2 to 5 Years of Age with Asthma: In> 1% of patients, was thirst. Pediatric Patients 6 Months to 2 Years of Age with Asthma: In > 1% of patients, were diarrhea, hyperkinesia, asthma, eczematous dermatitis and rash. Adults 15 Years of Age and Older with Seasonal Allergic Rhinitis: The incidence of somnolence. Pediatric Patients 2 to 14 Years of Age with Seasonal Allergic Rhinitis: No adverse reactions reported. Adults 15 Years of Age and Older with Perennial Allergic Rhinitis: The incidence of somnolence. Adults 15 Years of Age and Older with Asthma and Seasonal Allergic Rhinitis: The safety profile in asthmatic patients with seasonal allergic rhinitis was consistent with that observed in patients with asthma. PRESENTATION: 4-mg chewable tablet & 5-mg chewable tablet; 7 Chewable Tablets x 4 Blisters; 10-mg film-coated tablet; 7 Film coated Tablets x 4 Blisters. STORAGE CONDITIONS: Store the 4 mg and 5mg chewable tablets at 15-30°C, protected from moisture and light. Store 10mg film coated tablets below 30°C protected from moisture and light.
